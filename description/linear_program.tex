The linear program given in the following way:

We take the matrix
\[
A:= \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
31 & 24 & 78\\
63 & 17 & 35\\
99 & 19 & 34
\end{array}
\right)
\]
with entries in the rational numbers $\mathbb{Q}$.

Now we take the following two vector with entries in $\mathbb{Q}$:
\[
   b := (12, 51, 23)\ \mbox{ and }\ w := (64449, 26552, 73367).
\]

Now find $x\in\mathbb{Q}^3$ such that $A\cdot x\ge -b$ and $\langle x,w\rangle$
becomes minimal.
