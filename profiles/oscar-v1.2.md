Application profile for the OSCAR 1.2 serialization format. Relative to OSCAR
1.1, it adds most of the Lie-theory and polyhedral formats that were still
missing before, though matrix groups, abelian-group homomorphisms, and some
parameterized optimization examples remain unavailable.
